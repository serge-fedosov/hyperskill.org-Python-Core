There's a tuple containing hobbies of a person.

hobbies = ('reading', ['jogging', 'boxing', 'yoga'], 'movies', 'painting', ('photographing',))

  • Is it a correct tuple?
  • Can it be used as a dictionary key?
  • What is the result of the function len(hobbies)?

For the first two questions write yes or no, and for the third one write the number; separate the answers with a whitespace, so that a sample answer can look like no no 1.
